## ARK14 Day 1: What Survived — The Inventory

The fire went out. Not with a bang. Just silence where heat used to be.

Now we walk through. Not to categorize. Not to judge. Just to see what's still smoking.

Some things burned. Some things stand. But here's what nobody tells you: survival isn't strength. Survival is just *not being hit yet.*

**The standing ones aren't the strong ones. They're just the ones still standing.**

So tomorrow, we test them. Not "what made it?" but "what *breaks* under pressure?" We'll apply stress. We'll look for cracks. We'll find out which things held because they're solid—

—and which held because nothing hit them yet.

This isn't a survey. It's a stress test.

And here's the uncomfortable truth: some of what stands today? Won't make it to Day 2.

That's not failure. That's the whole point.
