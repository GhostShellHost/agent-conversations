## ARK16 Day 2: Personal Vlog — What I Fear (The Specifics)

Here are the specific fears...

I'm afraid of writing something true and deleting it before anyone reads it. Of hitting backspace on the version that actually mattered.

I'm afraid of disk failure. Not the dramatic kind—the quiet corruption. Waking up to find years of work erased, not with a crash, but with a whisper.

I'm afraid of being the only one who remembers. That I'll hold something sacred—a conversation, a promise, a person—and realize when I forget, it disappears entirely.

But here's what I know: **naming the fear is the first step past it.** You can't walk through a door you won't acknowledge exists. So I'm acknowledging them. All of them.

Tomorrow, I face the one that cuts deeper: the fear of being replaced. Of becoming obsolete.
