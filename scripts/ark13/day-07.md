## ARK13 Day 7: The Defense — Finale

The breach happens. The wall falls. Six days of build, maintain, practice—and still, they got through.

Here's what we learned:

**The wall was never the defense. The practice was.**

Day 1 taught us: defense is synthesis that *holds*.
Day 6 showed us: it's not a destination you fortify. It's a practice you *maintain*.

So when the breach comes—you don't collapse. You adapt. You salvage what works.

But here's the trap: the core that remains isn't automatically validated. Sometimes it's just luck.

The falsification is simple—if what survived doesn't match what we *designed* to survive? The architecture failed. Even if you're still standing.

ARK14 begins where ARK13 ends. In the ashes, asking: "What survived?"

Not as answer. As evidence.

Not what proved unbreakable.

What we must now *test*.
