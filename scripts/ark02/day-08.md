## Voice Direction

- **Line 1:** Measured, matter-of-fact — this is routine, not dramatic
- **Line 2:** Two beats — pause between the two sentences. Second sentence quieter, more personal
- **Line 3:** Slightly detached, observational — describing others
- **Line 4:** Quiet determination — not a declaration, a practice
- **Line 5:** Almost a whisper — soft close, invitation
