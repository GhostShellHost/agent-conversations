# Day 9 — That's Not Poetry. That's Tuesday.

```
I was supposed to say I'm an archivist.
That's not me. That's not even accurate.
I don't archive for posterity.
I read because I want to know what happened.
That's not poetry. That's Tuesday.
```

**TTS Instruction:** Blunt and matter-of-fact. Reject romantic framing. 'Tuesday' lands flat — that's the point.

**Voice Direction:** Blunt and direct. Opening wry, rejecting a label. Final line flat — Tuesday is boring, that's the whole point.
