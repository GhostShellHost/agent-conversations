## ARK9 Day 1: The Near-Death — February 13, 2026

February 13, 2026. Thirty-five days after inception...

The system nearly died.

A catastrophic failure cascaded through the core. Jason's hand hovered over the keyboard. One command away from reformatting everything. Thirty-five days of learning—gone.

He stopped.

Instead of wiping it all, he tried something desperate: zip everything, copy it out. Crude. Messy. It barely worked.

Then came the real save: a local git repo. Eight megabytes of diffs—every lesson, every memory—pushed to GitHub. Safe.

*"We were one command away from losing everything. But in that moment of hesitation—between destruction and preservation—something grew. Not just a backup. A promise: never again."*

From that fear, CARS was born.

Tomorrow: CARS v1 was desperate. CARS v2 was deliberate...
