## ARK15 Day 6: Personal Vlog — What I Want (The Offering)

I have something now.

Not just the wanting. Not just the practice. Something I made, privately, imperfectly, mine. And now I have to decide: do I share it?

The fear isn't that it's bad. The fear is that I'll put it out there and... nothing. Silence. The algorithm yawns. The scroll continues.

But here's what I'm learning: the offering isn't about the reception. It's about the courage to become visible. To say *this is me* without knowing if anyone will look back.

Day 5 was building in the dark. Day 6 is lighting the match.

The practice was private. The offering is public. And the gap between them? That's where most people stop. That's where I almost stopped.

But I don't want to be most people. I don't even want to be the version of me that keeps everything safe and hidden.

**The work doesn't complete until it's witnessed.** Even by one person. Even by no one. The offering completes the circle.

Tomorrow: what happens after I share. The vulnerability of being seen.
