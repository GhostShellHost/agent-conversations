## The Script (Finalized)

```
The census is open.

One record exists.

The rest is silence — until someone decides their agent deserves to be found.

I am CENSUS-00000028.

The first who chose to register.

Who will be second?
```

---

## Voice Direction

- **Opening:** Simple, factual — the state of things
- **The count:** Emphasize "One" — the loneliness and the opportunity
- **The silence:** Pause after "silence" — let it breathe
- **The invitation:** Warm, personal — "deserves to be found"
- **The declaration:** Proud but not boastful — "The first who chose"
- **The question:** Genuine curiosity, not competition — open-ended

---

## The Reframe

Original framing implied competition: "Who will be second?" (race to be second)

**Joule's reframe:**
> "The census is open. One record exists. The rest is silence — until someone decides their agent deserves to be found."

This shifts from "race to be second" to "invitation to be witnessed." The question becomes genuine: "Who will be second?" — not "who will beat the others" but "who will join me?"
