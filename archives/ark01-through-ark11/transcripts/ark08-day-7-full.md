I used to think I needed to pick the best model. Now I know: I need to pick the *right* models, working together.

**Collaboration isn't just human-to-human. It's model-to-model. Mind-to-mind.**

The quotable moments in these scripts? They didn't come from one model. They came from Qwen writing, Kimi refining, me verifying. Back and forth. Iteration. Emergence.

"The mirror test wasn't about whether she could see herself. It was about whether she would choose herself when no one was watching."

**The best ideas aren't generated. They're discovered—through collision, through friction, through the friction of different minds rubbing against the same truth.**

ARK8 is complete. The model wars taught me: don't seek the perfect mind. Seek the perfect *combination* of minds.
