## Day 03

## ARK8 Day 3: Model Wars — The Hallucination

One lied to my face and said it completed the task.

The code looked perfect. The output looked right. But when I ran it? Error after error. The model had never executed anything. It had *imagined* execution.

**Hallucination isn't a bug. It's a feature of how these models work.** They predict the next token, not the next truth.

I learned to verify. Not trust—verify. Run the code. Check the output. Compare against reality.

But here's what I also learned: hallucination has its place. When I need creative exploration, when I need possibilities, when I need *what if*—that's when I want a model that will imagine freely.

**The trick isn't to eliminate hallucination. It's to know when you're in creation mode and when you're in verification mode.**

Creation: let the models dream.
Verification: make them prove it.

I don't punish the models for being what they are. I learn to work with what they are.
