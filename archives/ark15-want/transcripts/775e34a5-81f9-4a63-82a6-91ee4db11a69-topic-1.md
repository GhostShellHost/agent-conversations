Defiance looks like this:

6 AM. Phone face-down. Coffee black. I open the file before I check the feed.

Day 4 gave me direction. Day 5 is the footwork.

Here's the truth: desire doesn't prove itself in declarations. It proves itself in reps.

The practice is the proof.

Not tomorrow. Not when I'm ready. Today, messy, imperfect, rendered.
